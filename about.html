About Our Chatbot

Our Chatbot has been around for awhile. Originally created in 2011, Our Chatbot has had a long time to learn many tricks. With every conversation, Our Chatbot learns something new. We hope it will grow with each month.

Our Chatbot is simple. It learns with everything said to it. When it speaks with a person, it remembers everything that person says. In conversation, Our Chatbot assumes everything the user says makes contextual sense. Therefore, if the user does not make sense with respect to what the chatbot says, Our Chatbot will not make any sense in the future. Spamming is not appreciated. For example, if Our Chatbot asks "How are you?" the user should respond accordingly. Whatever the user says in response will be recorded and used later as an output from Our Chatbot. If the user responds in our example with "I feel like I got ran over by Morgan Freeman's car," the next time someone asks Our Chatbot "How are you?" it may respond with "I feel like I got ran over by Morgan Freeman's car." If the user responds with something that makes no sense, Our Chatbot may still use it, and future users may think Our Chatbot is broken. Let's not break Our Chatbot. In this way, we teach Our Chatbot to speak. Please keep this in mind as you chat with Our Chatbot. We can make Our Chatbot better or worse; let's make it better!
